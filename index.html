Introduction

I am a PhD student in the Institute of Language Cognition and Computation at the University of Edinburgh supervised by Doctor Beatrice Alex and Doctor Alexandra Birch. I am a member of the CDT in Biomedical AI.

My research focuses on Large-Scale Multi-Label Document Classification in the medical domain in the task of Automatic ICD Coding of discharge summaries.

Prior to my PhD studies, I worked as a research engineer at Canon Medical Research Europe. My undergraduate degree was an integrated Master of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. A full CV is available upon request.

Research

My research focuses on incorporating external biomedical knowledge (e.g., the International Classification of Diseases ontology) into deep learning methods. This includes adjustments in data preparation (through augmentation and synthesis based on external knowledge), evaluation, and architecture-building.

Sunday Tea

Sunday Tea is an iniative designed to promote honest and open in-person communication.

The COVID-19 pandemic highlighted to me the issues of physical, mental, and emotional isolation. Coming back from the lockdowns, I would like to have a timeslot dedicated to understanding people — both others and myself. The spirit of the initiative is to say whatever needs to be said — be it your challenges with your PhD/work/daily life, your deep philosophical thoughts, or the most beautiful thing that happened to you last week. I cannot promise to understand, but I will do my best to listen.
